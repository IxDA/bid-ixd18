Bonjour

We want Interaction Week 18 to happen in Lyon

Let’s build the Frenchest, boldest and richest event possible!
This is our official submission, on behalf of IxDA Lyon and Villefontaine Design School

Lyon

First city for Culture, second City of the Arts in France, hometown of film-making, smart, food-savvy and sustainable. Lyon is the second biggest city in the country. Its vibrant heritage and energetic spirit convince 5.5 millions tourists every year to come and enjoy everything she has to offer.

We are convinced that Lyon is up for the challenge to host Interaction Week 18 in its lively atmosphere.

Discover Lyon

The bid

In this proposal, our focus is to make Interaction Week 2018 the best ever made. We took every design decision, from the theme we’d like to develop, to the locations we carefully handpicked in order to ensure a unique experience for our guests, while still remaining cost-effective.

Our main motivation is to show to the world that Lyon is at the cutting edge of interaction design!

Our vision for IxD week 2018

Why us?

Because a conference is a human matter, this submission was put together by 4 women and 4 men, who are dedicated to show how pivotal Lyon is when it comes to design. Our team also gets support from a great number of individuals and local organisations, who will help us to make a success of this event.

All in all, we definitely want to add a little challenge to our year 2018 and make it a bit more “spicy”!
