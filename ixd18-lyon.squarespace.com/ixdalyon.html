IxDA Lyon

 
 
 

Why?

Lyon’s interaction design community kicked off in 2008 at the instigation of Gilles Demarty and Thomas Di Luccio under the name “Les Designers Interactifs” (a French design association based in Paris). Inspired by this example, our two founders wanted to create a real local dynamic around interaction design in Lyon and to share their perspectives with local peers. 

Since the very beginning, the group was an IxDA chapter. It was in 2014 that “Designers Interactifs Lyon” became “IxDA Lyon” to further acknowledge the proximity with IxDA values and connect to the world! Since then, our members are involved in international meetings like Interaction conferences, UX Strat, UX barcamps and keep connecting across borders…

When & How

 

IxDA Lyon meetups are held the 3rd Thursday of each month as an after-work event. In line with the IxDA values, all our events are free and open to anyone who wants to join. 

As it is free (and we want to keep it that way), we find partners to host us. In exchange they introduce their activities and have an opportunity to connect with the design community. To make it valuable for everyone, we choose partners with a real interest in our participants and vice versa: co-working spaces, bars, theaters, agencies, labs, etc.

IxDA Lyon was incorporated as a non-profit organization in September 2015. From this point forward, the chapter has had a legal status, its own local board (elected every year) and a bank account to help its activities and sustainability grow.

 
 
 

Events & topics
