Mama Shelter Hotel

156 rooms — The Mama Shelter is a hot place right now. Located right outside the Presqu’île, on the east side of the Rhône banks, it features gorgeous rooms, and an appealing bar designed by Philippe Stark. The super attentive staff makes it a welcoming place in a laid-back atmosphere that is both stylish and playful. Staying there feels like staying at your hipster friends that decided to open a hotel. The bar is the place to be not only for its breakfast (Did we mention that it is a real feast?), but also for its excellent casual dinners or live concerts happening from Thursdays to Saturdays.

— Definitely the kind of hotel we would recommend to a friend! —

 
 

Sofitel

★★★★★ 164 rooms & suites — The Sofitel is a 5-star hotel located in the very center of Lyon, on the Presqu’île, 3 minutes away from Place Bellecour. Its Michelin-starred restaurant invites you to dine with a fantastic view over the city and its monuments. The atmosphere of this majestic place is warm and welcoming, the rooms offer sheer comfort.

Novotel Confluence

★★★★ 150 rooms — The hotel is located on the banks of the Saône River in the Confluence district – the former industrial port is now Lyon’s new trendy district, ultra modern buildings, cinemas, concert venues and leisure activities. Enjoy a drink on its terrasse bar with panoramic view. A water taxi stops right in front of the hotel and takes you directly to the old city center.
